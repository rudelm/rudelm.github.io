## Introduction
Apple offered this year a new version of Pages and Numbers. It is now an universal app and Apple [urges](https://support.apple.com/en-md/126151) you to update it whenever possible. I've installed the newer versions, because I was unable to synchronize changes to a numbers table stored in my iCloud account.

## The problem
What happens when you're annoyed with the newest version and want to go back to an older version? The macOS App Store is the source for installing the apps. However, I do not see results for Numbers or Pages pointing to the older version.

## The solution
I've found the links via a search machine and wanted to link here directly to the App Store entries, in case I'll need them in the future:

 * [Pages 14.5](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/pages-14-5/id409201541?mt=12)
 * [Numbers 14.5](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/numbers-14-5/id409203825?mt=12)

## Conclusion
Apple hides these entries. Without the direct link or ID, you're unable to download these versions again. It's probably just a matter of time until they are completely gone from the App Store. But at least until then, there's a way to get them back.
